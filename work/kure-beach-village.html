Kure Beach Village

This is the community website for Kure Beach Village Homeowners Association and its 350+ families. It was built using Wordpress, which I customized with my own theme. I also wrote a module that is used to search and display information to logged in users from the association owners directory. For several years I have been the webmaster for this site. I maintain the site, apply Wordpress updates, add new content and features as needed, and send weekly email updates to homeowner subscribers.
