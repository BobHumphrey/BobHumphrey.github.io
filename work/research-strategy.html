Research Strategy

Resources at Randall Library include over 500,000 books, 34,000 e-books, 93,000 bound periodical volumes, and 52,000 online journals. Helping students get access to all of that information is an ongoing challenge. It takes more than a Google search to locate the best resources for academic work. Research Strategy is an application that helps students find articles and books appropriate for serious research. It walks the student through a step-by-step process for creating an effective search statement and then helps the student record and save the search results.
